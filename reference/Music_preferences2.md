# Music Preference by College Major

Fictional data, with more extreme preferences than
[Music_preferences](https://easystats.github.io/effectsize/reference/Music_preferences.md)

## Format

A 4-by-3 table, with a *column* for each major and a *row* for each type
of music.

    data("Music_preferences2")
    Music_preferences2
    #>       Pop Rock Jazz Classic
    #> Psych 151  130   12       7
    #> Econ   77    6  111       4
    #> Law     0    4    2     165
